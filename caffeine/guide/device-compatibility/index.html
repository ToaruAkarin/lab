# Xiaomi (MIUI)

Due to MIUI's strict background limit, the tile might not be lighted. you need to turn off battery saver for Caffeine:

  • Disable Caffeine's Hide icon option temporarily
  • Open Security app
  • Choose Battery
  • Click "" icon
  • Choose App battery saver
  • Click Caffeine
  • Click No restrictions
